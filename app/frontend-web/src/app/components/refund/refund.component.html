A.) Cancellation/Return and Refund Policy:

1) Multiple times debiting of Customer's Card/Bank Account due to technical error OR Customer's account being debited with excess amount in a single transaction due to technical error. In such cases, excess amount excluding Payment Gateway Charges (if any) would be refunded to the Customer.

2) Cancellation of a Service requested for:

If the cancellation is done on the same day, then the calculated amount to be refunded will be refunded within 3 working days.

a) Fee + Taxes paid to the Government Department cannot be refunded if the Challan has been generated.
b) In case if your application is rejected by the Government Department for whatever reason(s) they might deem fit, then no refund whatsoever is applicable.
c) Service fee collected could be refunded only partially – on a case-to-case basis depending on the processing stage reached. Then the amount that has to be refunded, will be refunded within 28 calendar days.

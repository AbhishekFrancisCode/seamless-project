Everyone avails services offered by the government departments frequently – particularly from the RTO – be it obtaining a learners license or a driving license, registering a vehicle or its transfer to someone else. Apart from these there are a few of other services which are offered and availed. With the deployment of technology, many of the government departments have gone digital – including the RTO. While this eliminates the need to physically go to the jurisdictional RTO office to obtain a service, the need to fill-up online forms still remains and a lot of people including the corporates are not so comfortable filling-up those forms – owing to lack of knowledge – in many cases.
Seamless Online Services has been formed to help clients overcome these hassles.

We make RTO services available to the common man in his locality – at his doorstep,
through online service delivery, and we ensure, efficiency, transparency, and reliability of these services at affordable costs.

We are a professional agency and we discharge our duties reliably with a sense of accountability.
We propose to offer government services in general and RTO services in particular to begin with.
